## Management Principles

### 1. Simplification Principle
- Remove unnecessary complexity
- Keep project structure clear
- Focus on core functionality

### 2. Consistency Principle
- Unified component naming conventions
- Consistent functionality definitions
- Standardized development processes

### 3. Efficiency Principle
- Reduce duplicate work
- Improve development efficiency
- Optimize resource usage

---

## Decision Process

Project management decisions follow this process:

1. **Problem Identification**: Clearly identify problems to be solved
2. **Impact Analysis**: Assess decision impact on project
3. **Solution Comparison**: Compare pros and cons of different solutions
4. **Team Discussion**: Internal team discussion and review
5. **Decision Recording**: Record decision process and results
6. **Execution Tracking**: Track decision execution effectiveness

---

## Decision Impact

Each project decision records:

- **Decision Context**: Why this decision is needed
- **Impact Scope**: Modules and functions affected by decision
- **Execution Plan**: Specific execution steps and timeline
- **Risk Assessment**: Possible risks and countermeasures
- **Success Criteria**: How to evaluate decision success
